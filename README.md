The Project APCity Prayaanam is a web app developed primarily to test the feasibility and practicality of developing a full pledged app to track city in Andhraparadesh.
The Prayaanam in telugu means travel, city prayaanam implies travelling in city. Due to expansion of the city the demand for public transport is increasing. 
The app aims to give a picture of tracking buses location, occupancy and also e-pass system.
